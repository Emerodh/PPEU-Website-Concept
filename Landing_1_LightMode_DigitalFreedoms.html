DIGITAL FREEDOM IS THE FOUNDATION OF 21ST CENTURY DEMOCRACY

We're fighting for an open, fair and user-controlled digital society where privacy is a fundamental right and where people, not corporations, own their data.

JOIN THE MOVEMENT LEARN MORE

KEY POLICIES

PRIVACY PROTECTION

We believe encryption and data ownership are fundamental rights. No government or corporation should have unrestricted access to your personal data or communications.

Read Our Position →

TRANSPARENT GOVERNANCE

Democracy requires informed citizens. We advocate for radical transparency in government operations, algorithmic decision-making, and corporate influence in politics.

Read Our Position →

COPYRIGHT REFORM

The current copyright system stifles innovation and creativity. We support fair use, reduced copyright terms, and expanded public domain to enable a flourishing digital commons.

Read Our Position →

STOP THE SURVEILLANCE STATE

Our current campaign aims to end mass surveillance programs across Europe. We're pushing for comprehensive privacy legislation that would require judicial oversight for all surveillance and ban mass data collection.
