DEMOCRATIZING THE DIGITAL ECONOMY IS THE KEY TO SHARED PROSPERITY

We're building an economic future where innovation is open, wealth is distributed fairly, and citizens—not corporations—shape the rules of our digital markets.

JOIN THE MOVEMENT LEARN MORE

ECONOMIC POLICIES

DIGITAL ECONOMY FAIRNESS

The concentration of market power in few digital platforms threatens innovation and economic freedom. We advocate for breaking up digital monopolies, enforcing platform neutrality, and ensuring that workers in the digital economy receive fair compensation and benefits.

Read Our Position →

UNIVERSAL BASIC INCOME

Economic security is a prerequisite for true freedom. We support implementing a Universal Basic Income that provides every citizen economic stability, encourages entrepreneurship, and recognizes unpaid contributions to society like care work and community service.

Read Our Position →

OPEN INNOVATION ECONOMICS

The current patent and copyright system blocks progress and concentrates wealth. We promote open source development, shorter intellectual property terms, and transparent funding for public research that benefits all citizens, not just shareholders.

Read Our Position →

BREAK THE PLATFORM MONOPOLIES

Our current economic campaign targets the monopolistic practices of major tech platforms. We're pushing for legislation that would prevent digital gatekeepers from self-preferencing, ensure data portability, and create a level playing field for innovative startups and small businesses across Europe.
